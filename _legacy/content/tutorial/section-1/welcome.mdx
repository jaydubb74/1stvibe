# Welcome — Here's What You're About to Build

You're going to build a real, live website. Not a mockup. Not a template. A page that lives on the actual internet, with a URL you can text to your friends or put in your email signature.

You'll do it using AI — specifically, a tool called **Claude Code** — by describing what you want in plain English.

Here's what the finished product looks like:

> A personal webpage with your name, a short bio, your favorite things, links to your social profiles, and a clean, mobile-friendly design. Something you'd actually want to show people.

## What you'll need

- About **60–90 minutes** — most of it watching AI do the work
- A **Mac or Windows PC** with internet access (Mac is a bit smoother today, but Windows works great)
- An **email address** — you'll create two free accounts along the way

**Zero coding experience** — seriously, none

## How to Use This Tutorial

The checklist on the left tracks every step of the journey — each one gets a checkmark when you're done. Here on the right is where you'll find the instructions, screenshots, and explanations.

When you finish a step, hit **Mark complete** at the bottom and move to the next one.
